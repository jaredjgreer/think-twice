HOW TO PLAY

⬡ SETUP

Create players with your icon & birthday. Your age sets your difficulty tier (Easy / Medium / Hard).

Pick one or more decks — mix them for variety! Choose your grid size and game mode.

With 4+ players you can form teams — valid team sizes light up based on your player count.

⬡ GAMEPLAY

Take turns flipping cards on the grid. Each card hides a challenge question.

You have a countdown timer to answer — if it runs out, that counts as wrong!

Don't want to answer? Hit SKIP to flip the card back, but you'll lose your streak and your turn.

Answer correctly = +50 pts. Wrong = the card flips back and the next player can STEAL for 25 pts. If they miss, the pot grows and the next player gets a shot!

⬡ SPECIAL CARDS

Cards have hidden rarities — you won't know until you flip!

✧ RARE — Worth 2× points (100 pts)!

★ WILD — Worth 75 pts for a surprise twist!

☠ SABOTAGE — Bumps you to a harder difficulty tier!

⬡ STREAKS & CROWN

Answer correctly in a row to build a 🔥 STREAK. The higher your streak, the shorter your timer gets (60s → 50s → 40s → 30s minimum).

The player in the lead wears the 👑 CROWN. Take the lead and you'll see the DETHRONED! animation. Hold the crown to flex on everyone!

⬡ BONUS

Complete a full row, column, or diagonal on the grid for a tic-tac-toe bonus (+25 pts per card in the line)!

The player with the most points at the end wins!

WHAT IS THINK TWICE?

A brain-training card game that teaches you to recognize cognitive biases, emotional intelligence skills, gospel principles, professional concepts, and more — while competing with friends and family!
